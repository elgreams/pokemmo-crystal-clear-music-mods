# pokemmo-crystal-clear-music-mods
Mods for PokéMMO that adds music from Crystal Clear 

### Music Coverage
Depending on the generation, mods may include:
- Wild battle music  
- Trainer battle music  
- Gym battle music  
- Surf music  
- Bike music  
- PokéCenter music  

Some generations may include **only battle themes**.

## Installation

1. Download the .mod file(s) for the generation(s) you want.
2. Place them in your PokéMMO `mods` folder.
3. Enable the mods in the PokéMMO mod manager.
4. Restart the game if needed.

(These are standard PokéMMO audio mods — no special setup required.)
